White cyclorama 7*6 meters. All the walls are white, which gives many opportunities to work with reflected light. There is also bright daylight in the hall. The second part of the hall has a concrete floor.The only decoration is a 3.5 meters long and 2.5 meters high stainless steel wavy silver screen, which will be a great decoration for your shooting. There is also a stylish leather chair in the hall. For additional furniture/props, contact our administrators, you can view some of the props on our website. – The area is 70 square meters. – The ceiling height is 4.5 m.– Cyclorama, 6m wide– Distance - 6 m– Natural light - four large windows.– Blackout blinds.– Rail for things.– Air conditioning.– Wi-Fi. *For each person outside the limit, an additional payment of 150 UAH
